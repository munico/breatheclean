Who We Are

The Breathe Clean Association was founded by the knowledge that those who live in less fortunate areas of the world suffer the most through air pollution--though most of the times it is caused by first world countries. A lack of resources, education, and inadequate healthcare puts these people under severe risk, and they have been left unprotected.


What We Aim To Accomplish

Our strive is to bring awareness to the detrimental affects air pollution has on the third-world, not just the humans but all other forms of nature. To do so we aim to organize fundraisers, petitions, protests, food-drives, voluntary work, and much more in order to help balance the scale of which our world is dealing with climate change. We are a non-profit organization, and all proceeds and donations are sent to those in need.
